## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A valid business email address
- Access to your SAT credentials (RFC, e.firma or password)
- Basic details about your first shipment (origin, destination, client info)
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
Embarque supports unlimited users from day one. No contracts required.
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [https://embarque.mx](https://embarque.mx) and click **Agendar demo** or **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your email, company name, and phone number. Submit the form to receive your invitation link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Embarque. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to [https://app.embarque.mx](https://app.embarque.mx). Enter your email and set your password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Initial Dashboard Setup

Customize your dashboard for your logistics operation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect SAT Credentials" icon="settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > SAT Integration**.

    Enter your RFC and upload your e.firma or CSD password. Test the connection to ensure CFDI 4.0 and Carta Porte 3.1 compliance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Roles" icon="users">
    Go to **Team > Users**.

    Invite team members by email. Assign roles like **Admin**, **Dispatcher**, or **Accountant** for embarque creation, invoicing, and collections.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Company Profile" icon="building">
    In **Settings > Company**, add your business address, logo, and default payment terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Embarque

Generate your first shipment and invoice it seamlessly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Embarque" icon="truck">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Shipment" icon="plus">
        Click **New Embarque** on the dashboard. Enter origin (`<Monterrey>`), destination (`<Mexico City>`), weight (`<500kg>`), and client details.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Generate Carta Porte" icon="file-text">
        Review shipment details. Click **Generate CFDI** to create SAT-compliant invoice with Carta Porte 3.1.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Send to Client" icon="send">
        Share the PDF invoice via email or WhatsApp integration. Track status in **Cobranza**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Bulk Upload" icon="upload">
    For multiple shipments, use **Imports > Excel Template**.

    Download the template, fill with shipment data, and upload. Embarque processes and generates invoices automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Upload Initial Documents

Organize your existing data quickly.

<Expandable title="How to Bulk Upload Historical Embarques" default-open="true">
  1. Prepare an Excel file with columns: Client, Origin, Destination, Date, Amount.
  2. Go to **Data > Imports**.
  3. Select **Historical Embarques** and upload your file.
  4. Map columns and preview. Confirm to import.

  <Callout kind="success">
    Imports support up to 1,000 rows. 98% SAT acceptance on first try.
  </Callout>
</Expandable>
